# SafariPlus Competitive Strategy: How to Beat the OTAs

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** January 2026
**Purpose:** Strategic roadmap to outcompete Viator, GetYourGuide, SafariBookings, and TourRadar in East African safari market

---

## Executive Summary

SafariPlus has two unfair advantages that no major OTA can easily replicate:

1. **Native M-Pesa Integration** - No global OTA supports East African mobile money
2. **Lower Commission Rates** - 10-15% vs 20-25% industry standard

However, advantages alone don't win markets. This document outlines the specific improvements needed to convert these advantages into market dominance.

**Goal:** Become the #1 safari booking platform in East Africa within 24 months.

---

## Part 1: Know Your Enemy

### Competitor Breakdown

| Platform | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Opportunity |
|----------|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| **Viator** | Brand trust, 500k+ reviews, massive traffic | 25% commission, no M-Pesa, not Africa-focused | Lower fees, local payments |
| **GetYourGuide** | Modern UX, strong mobile app, global reach | 20-25% commission, day-tour focused, no M-Pesa | Multi-day safaris, M-Pesa |
| **SafariBookings** | Safari-specific, strong SEO, 16k+ tours | Lead-gen only (no booking), no payments | Full booking capability |
| **TourRadar** | Multi-day focus, good reviews | 20-25% commission, no local payments | Lower fees, M-Pesa |

### What OTAs Do Better Than Us (Currently)

1. **Trust & Social Proof** - They have millions of reviews, we have zero
2. **Brand Recognition** - "Viator" is a verb, "SafariPlus" is unknown
3. **SEO Authority** - They rank #1 for every safari keyword
4. **Mobile Apps** - Native iOS/Android apps with offline support
5. **24/7 Support** - Live chat, phone support, instant responses
6. **Content Marketing** - Thousands of destination guides and articles
7. **Personalization** - AI-powered recommendations based on behavior
8. **Inventory** - Thousands of tours vs our starting inventory

### What We Do Better Than Them (Our Moat)

1. **M-Pesa/Mobile Money** - They literally cannot accept this
2. **Commission Rates** - 10-15% vs their 20-25%
3. **Agent Tools** - Superior dashboard and management features
4. **Regional Focus** - Built for East Africa, not an afterthought
5. **Agility** - We can ship features in days, they take months

// Automatic gateway selection based on payment method and customer location
const selectGateway = (method: string, country: string) => {
  switch (method) {
    case 'MPESA':
      return 'pesapal'  // Only Pesapal supports M-Pesa

    case 'CARD':
      // Use Pesapal for East African cards, Flutterwave for international
      if (['KE', 'TZ', 'UG', 'RW'].includes(country)) {
        return 'pesapal'
      }
      return 'flutterwave'

    case 'PAYPAL':
      return 'paypal'

    case 'BANK_TRANSFER':
      return 'wise'

    default:
      return 'flutterwave'
  }
}
